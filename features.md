# Foxtrot Mod - Feature List

## 🖼️ HUD System
- **Dynamic Offset Engine**: Real-time column alignment that adapts to name lengths.
- **Visual Editor**: Click-and-drag positioning with live resizing and double-click configuration.
- **Modules**:
    - **Enemy/Friend/Teammate Lists**: Real-time tracking of specific player groups.
    - **Nicked List**: Tracks potential "nicked" players in the lobby.
    - **Reg/Darks HUD**: Specialized tracking for high-tier Pit gear users.
    - **Potion/Armor/Coords**: Clean, customizable status overlays.
    - **Session Stats**: Tracks XP, gold, and performance per session.

## 👁️ ESP & Visuals
- **Multi-Color Highlighting**:
    - **Red**: Enemies.
    - **Green**: Friends.
    - **Cyan**: Teammates & Guild members.
- **Skeleton Highlighter**: Shows player poses and limb positions through walls.
- **High-Performance Tracking**: Uses Netty packet interception for zero-lag Dragon Egg and event tracking.

## 🔍 Denicking & Identification
- **Auto-Denick**: Automatically scrapes Hypixel API/History to identify nicked players.
- **UUID Persistence**: Tracks players by UUID to ensure they remain identified even across name changes.
- **PitPal Integration**: Synchronizes guild rosters automatically.

## ⚔️ Combat & Automation
- **Auto-Clicker**: Safe, randomized clicking with inventory-fill and block-breaking support.
- **ChestStealer**: Blazing fast chest looting with GUI-safe toggling.
- **W-Tap**: Automated distancing for optimized PvP combos.
- **Auto-Ghead**: Automatic Golden Head consumption based on health.
- **Auto-PantSwap**: Intelligent pant swapping for Venom/Defense tactics.
- **Auto-BulletTime**: Automated blocking/dodging logic for projectiles.

## 🛠️ Utilities
- **Dead Lobby Finder**: Finds and warps you into low-player lobbies for safe grinding.
- **Ring Helper**: Assists with the Pit "The Ring" event.
- **Focus Mode**: Hides all irrelevant players to improve FPS and focus.
- **Rank/Name Spoofing**: Customize your local appearance.
- **Venom Timer**: Track your venom cooldowns precisely.
